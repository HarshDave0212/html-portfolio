The Best Movies According to Harsh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

URI

Uri's movie revolves around the surgical strike the Indian army underwent in 2006 after the attack on Uri, a town in Jammu and Kashmir.

Scam 1992

The rise and fall of Harshad Mehta, a stockbroker who single-handedly took the stock market to great heights.

Drishyam

Story of a common family man who takes desperate measures to save his wife and two daughters.
